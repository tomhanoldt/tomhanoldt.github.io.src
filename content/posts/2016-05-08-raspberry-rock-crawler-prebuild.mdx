# Raspberry - Rock Crawler - prebuild

Here is the new raspberry experiment based on a cheap Rock-Crawler. My first experiment with a vehicle build on a tank ([you know](http://blog.tomhanoldt.info/code/javascript/raspberry-pi-tank)) had problems with a weak motor shield.
So i started a new experiment based on a cheap (35€) remote controlled Rock-Crawler from [Real](http://onlineshop.real.de/R-C-Fahrzeug-2-4-GHz-Rock-Crawler/0330258001001) with front and heck motor. I added a more powerful [battery](https://www.conrad.de/de/modellbau-akkupack-nimh-72-v-2000-mah-conrad-energy-stick-tamiya-stecker-206025.html) (18€) and stronger [absorber](https://wwwsecure.us.lego.com/de-de/service/replacementparts/sale) (4\*2,33€ - Nr. 6083112) not only for fun but also ;) 
At the end the vehicle will transport a battery for motor and one for the raspberry, a motor shield, webcam and w-lan module. This pre build version is for research and preparation.
The first step was to mount a new battery for more speed and stabilization of the absorber by replacing the springs. For balance reasons you can cutoff some rods from the chassis and center the battery.
